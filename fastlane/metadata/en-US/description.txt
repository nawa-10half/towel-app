When did you last replace your household items?

Towels, toothbrushes, sponges, sheets, water filters... We use them every day, so keeping them fresh matters. But how often do you forget when you last replaced them?

Kaetao automatically tracks replacement schedules for all your household items. Just log when you replace something, and the app reminds you when it's time for the next change. Never forget a replacement again.

EASY REPLACEMENT TRACKING
Register your towels, toothbrushes, kitchen sponges, bed sheets, filters, and more. Set custom replacement intervals and see at a glance which items need changing with clear status indicators.

AI TOWEL CONDITION CHECK
Take a photo of your towel, and AI evaluates fading, stains, pilling, and fraying on a 100-point scale. Let AI help you decide: "Still good to use?" or "Time for a new one?"

REMINDER NOTIFICATIONS
Get notified when it's time to replace your items. Stay on top of household hygiene even on your busiest days.

FAMILY GROUP SHARING
Invite family members with an invite code and share item management. Keep the whole family on track for a clean, organized home.
